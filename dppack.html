About our history

1945 – 1979 In 1945 our grand father Hassan Ghafourzadeh Nobar laid the foundations for our modern , fully equipped packaging company in Tabriz.

We look back with pleasure and pride on our 71 year company history , so welcome to the journey throw time that shows what can be achieved with the strong will and strength of innovation.

1979 As a family owned company in the second generation ,Rahim Ghafourzadeh Nobar did know how important it is to maintain quality and at the same time fulfil the wishes of the customers ,therefore he expanded the company in the new industrial area to 12000 square meters in building.

He has always invested in modern and high tech machinery in which substantially has lead to significant increment in quantity and also quality.his main aim is to surpass the expectations of our customers with regard to reliability,flexibility and quality.

2014 Globalization and digitalization makes the world smaller and also expand the business territory of Derakhshan pack ,the third generation of the family Zohreh & Fereshteh Ghafourzadeh Nobar joined the company with academic start from universities in united kingdom, Germany and Iran, which will brings the benefit to our customers.

Our Services



Laminated Metallized Film Print & Folding Carton Packaging & Design

Folding cartons continue to dominate
the packaging world. Their beautiful printability, ability to convert from
flat storage to complex shapes and affordability make them the smar choice for products from every industry.
At Derakhshan this means our job is even more important to customers.
How do you get the most from this popular form of packaging?

Rigid Box Manufacturing and Design

We design and manufacture rigid boxes for a variety of industries. We fabricate everything from raw board boxes to fine laminated boxes for the cosmetics and specialty packaging world. We craft branded packaging for crystal, glassware, jewelers, candy companies, fashion designers, department stores and more. When you want to express quality and distinction, the rigid box is hard to beat.

Printing & Specialty Finishing

Printing, Specialty Finishing, Coatings and Laminates. The list of packaging possibilities with ink, stock surfaces, imagery, embossing, foils, can often create more confusion than clarity. In the hands of a great packaging printer, state-of-the-art equipment and creative minds, printing can achieve extraordinary packaging. Metallics, fluorescents, satin, gloss, shimmer and more are all possible.

Graphic & Structural Design & Photography Studio

Our full-service graphic design
group is here to simplify your job, prepare prototypes, create concepts and ensure that the right decisions are made by asking the right questions.The shape and contour of a package or display helps to capture the shopper's attention and are even trademarked as part of some brands. Our structural design capabilities are available to all customers.
